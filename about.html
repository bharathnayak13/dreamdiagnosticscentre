About Us

Dream Diagnostic Centre is a leading diagnostic facility based in Karkala, Karnataka, offering a wide range of reliable and affordable medical tests. With a 4.9+ star rating on Google and the trust of thousands, we are committed to accuracy, quality, and compassionate care.

From advanced imaging and pathology services to routine health checkups, our experienced team and modern infrastructure ensure timely, precise results for every patient.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and accurate diagnostic services that empower individuals and support better health outcomes in our community.

Our Vision

To be Karkala’s most trusted diagnostic partner — blending technology, expertise, and human care to build a healthier tomorrow.

Core Values

  • Accuracy: We uphold the highest quality in diagnostic testing.
  • Affordability: Healthcare should be within reach for all.
  • Care: Every patient is treated with dignity and empathy.
  • Integrity: Transparency and honesty are our foundation.
  • Innovation: We invest in technology that improves lives.
